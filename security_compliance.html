Certification Phases

Phase 1: India Launch (Mandatory)

üáÆüá≥ Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act

Avoids penalties up to ‚Çπ250 Crore. Required to operate legally in India.

Required Documents:
  • Privacy Notice: Itemized list of data collected (English + Regional).
  • Consent Artifacts: Digital logs of "affirmative action" consent.
  • Data Processing Agreement (DPA): Contract with clients.
  • Electronic Consent Form: "I withdraw consent" mechanism.
Entity Documents:
  • Certificate of Incorporation (CIN)
  • PAN & TAN Cards
  • GST Registration Certificate
  • MSME Udyam Registration (Optional, for benefits)
